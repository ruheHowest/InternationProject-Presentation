Step 1 — The Intelligent Cabin

Collect health data invisibly — no cameras, privacy-first design.

Force sensors in the cushion detect poor posture.

Haptic Steering Wheel

Conductive fabric measures heart rate and grip strength to detect stress spikes in real time.

Step 2 — The "Bio-Data" Handshake

Data stays with the driver. The employer never sees personal health data.

🔒

Edge Computing

All biometric data is processed locally on the truck's edge device. Nothing goes to the cloud or the employer.

🧬

The Digital Twin

A temporary virtual model of the driver's physical state is created on the fly.

06 / 08

Step 3 — The Recovery Pod

A private micro-lounge that physically adapts to the data received from the truck.

🪞

Smart Mirror

Visualizes the driver's "stiffness map" and guides them through 3 specific stretches to release tension detected during the drive.

💡

Adaptive Environment

Lighting shifts to cool blues & greens (chromotherapy) if high stress was detected on the road.

💆

Targeted Massage

The lounge chair activates massage rollers only on the specific muscle groups that were overworked that day.

🌡️

Climate Control

Temperature and humidity adjust based on the driver's detected fatigue and stress levels for optimal relaxation.

🧘

Guided Breathing

Audio-visual breathing exercises sync with ambient lighting to bring heart rate down after high-stress drives.
